# HealthGuard Insurance — Policy Definitions

_FictiHealth Inc. | Policy Year 2024_

---

## Key Terms

**Deductible:** The amount you pay out-of-pocket for covered health-care services before your insurance
plan begins to pay. Applies per policy year. Family deductibles work on an aggregate basis.

**Coinsurance:** Your share of costs of a covered health-care service, calculated as a percentage of
the allowed amount for the service, after you've paid your deductible.

**Copay:** A fixed amount you pay for a covered health-care service, usually when you receive the
service.

**Out-of-Pocket Maximum (OOP Max):** The most you pay for covered services in a plan year. After you
reach this amount, your insurance pays 100% of covered services for the rest of the year.

**Allowed Amount:** Maximum amount your plan pays for a covered health-care service. May also be called
"eligible expense," "payment allowance," or "negotiated rate."

**Covered Service:** A health-care service, treatment, or prescription drug covered under your plan.

**Excluded Service:** A health-care service that your plan does not cover and for which you are
financially responsible.

**In-Network Provider:** A provider or facility that has contracted with your insurance plan to provide
services at a negotiated rate.

**Out-of-Network Provider:** A provider or facility that has not contracted with your plan. Higher
cost-sharing typically applies.

**Prior Authorization (PA):** Approval from your insurer required before you receive certain services,
medications, or equipment. Failure to obtain PA may result in denial of coverage.

**Medically Necessary:** Services that are appropriate and consistent with the diagnosis or treatment
of a condition and consistent with generally accepted standards of medical practice.

**Preventive Care:** Routine health-care services (screenings, check-ups, patient counseling) aimed at
preventing illness, disease, or health problems.

**Formulary:** A list of prescription drugs covered by your plan.
