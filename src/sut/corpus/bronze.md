# HealthGuard Bronze Plan — Benefit Summary

_FictiHealth Inc. | Policy Year 2024_

---

## §1. Overview

The Bronze plan is our most affordable option for members who want protection against high medical
costs while keeping monthly premiums low. Best suited for generally healthy individuals who
anticipate minimal routine care.

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Monthly Premium | $280 |
| Annual Deductible (individual) | $4,000 |
| Annual Deductible (family) | $8,000 |
| Out-of-Pocket Maximum (individual) | $8,000 |
| Out-of-Pocket Maximum (family) | $16,000 |
| Coinsurance (after deductible) | 40% member / 60% plan |

---

## §2. Preventive Care

Preventive services are covered at **100%** with no deductible when provided by an in-network
provider:

- Annual wellness exam
- Recommended immunizations
- Colorectal cancer screenings
- Mammography (per USPSTF guidelines)
- Blood pressure, cholesterol, and diabetes screenings

---

## §3. Medical Benefits (after deductible, unless noted)

### §3.1 Primary Care Visits
- **In-network:** $50 copay per visit (deductible waived for first 3 visits per year)
- **Out-of-network:** 40% coinsurance after deductible

### §3.2 Specialist Visits
- **In-network:** $90 copay per visit
- **Out-of-network:** 40% coinsurance after deductible

### §3.3 Emergency Room
- **$350 copay** per visit (waived if admitted). Deductible applies before copay on some plans;
  on Bronze the copay is the only member obligation for ER visits regardless of deductible status.

### §3.4 Surgical Benefits
The following surgical procedures are covered at **60% plan / 40% member** after the deductible is
satisfied, when medically necessary and prior-authorized:

- Appendectomy
- Cholecystectomy (gallbladder removal)
- Knee and hip arthroplasty
- Bariatric surgery (gastric bypass and sleeve gastrectomy)
- Hernia repair
- Cataract surgery

### §3.5 Inpatient Hospital
- Room and board: 40% coinsurance after deductible (in-network)
- ICU/CCU: 40% coinsurance after deductible
- Prior authorization required for non-emergency admissions

### §3.6 Mental Health and Substance Use
- Inpatient: 40% coinsurance after deductible
- Outpatient/therapy: $60 copay per visit (up to 26 visits per year)

## §5. Lab and Imaging

- **Diagnostic labs:** 40% coinsurance after deductible (in-network)
- **X-rays:** $60 copay (in-network)
- **MRI / CT / PET:** 40% coinsurance after deductible; **prior authorization required**
