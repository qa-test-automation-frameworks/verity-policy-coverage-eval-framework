# HealthGuard Insurance — Exclusions and Limitations

_FictiHealth Inc. | Policy Year 2024_

---

## Services Not Covered (All Plans)

The following services are **explicitly excluded** from coverage under all HealthGuard plans regardless
of tier. Members are solely responsible for the cost of excluded services.

1. **Cosmetic procedures** — services intended solely to improve appearance, including rhinoplasty,
   blepharoplasty, liposuction, and elective breast augmentation. Reconstructive surgery following
   a medically necessary procedure is covered.

2. **Experimental and investigational treatments** — services, drugs, or procedures not approved by
   the FDA or not meeting FictiHealth's clinical evidence standards.

3. **Bariatric surgery (gastric bypass and sleeve gastrectomy)** — weight-loss surgical procedures
   are excluded under all plans. Nutritional counseling for medically documented obesity is covered
   under Silver and Gold.

4. **Dental care** — routine dental exams, cleanings, fillings, extractions, and orthodontia are
   excluded unless a separate dental rider is purchased.

5. **Vision correction** — eyeglasses, contact lenses, and LASIK surgery are excluded unless a
   separate vision rider is purchased. Annual eye exams are excluded (preventive dental/vision is
   not covered under the base health plan).

6. **Long-term custodial care** — assistance with daily living activities (bathing, dressing, eating)
   in a nursing facility or at home.

7. **Fertility treatments** — IVF, IUI, egg/sperm freezing, and related services.

8. **Weight-loss medications** — prescription medications whose primary indication is weight loss.

9. **Services outside scope of the policy** — medical advice, diagnoses, surgical recommendations, or
   second opinions on whether you should undergo a specific procedure. This plan covers costs, not
   decisions. Members seeking guidance on whether to obtain surgery, which provider to use, or what
   treatment is best should consult a licensed medical professional.

---

## Lifetime and Annual Limits

- No lifetime dollar limits apply to essential health benefits.
- Skilled nursing facility stays are limited to **90 days per calendar year** (all plans).
- Home health aide visits are limited to **60 visits per calendar year** (all plans).
- Physical therapy: up to **30 sessions per calendar year** (Bronze); **45 sessions** (Silver); **60 sessions** (Gold).

---

## Prior Authorization Requirements

The following require PA for all plans. Failure to obtain PA results in denial of the claim:

- Inpatient hospital admissions (non-emergency)
- Outpatient surgery (non-emergency)
- MRI, CT, PET scans
- Home health services
- Durable medical equipment over $500
- Specialty drugs (Tier 4+)
