# HealthGuard Gold Plan — Benefit Summary

_FictiHealth Inc. | Policy Year 2024_

---

## §1. Overview

The Gold plan provides comprehensive coverage with low cost-sharing, making it ideal for members who
anticipate significant health-care utilization.

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Monthly Premium | $580 |
| Annual Deductible (individual) | $750 |
| Annual Deductible (family) | $1,500 |
| Out-of-Pocket Maximum (individual) | $4,000 |
| Out-of-Pocket Maximum (family) | $8,000 |
| Coinsurance (after deductible) | 10% member / 90% plan |

---

## §2. Preventive Care

Preventive services at **100%** with no deductible, in-network. Includes all Silver preventive
benefits plus:

- Colonoscopy (per USPSTF guidelines, no additional cost)
- Annual skin cancer screening

---

## §3. Medical Benefits

### §3.1 Primary Care Visits
- **In-network:** $20 copay per visit (deductible waived — copay applies from first visit)
- **Out-of-network:** 10% coinsurance after deductible

### §3.2 Specialist Visits
- **In-network:** $40 copay per visit
- **Out-of-network:** 10% coinsurance after deductible

### §3.3 Emergency Room
- **$150 copay** per visit; waived if admitted; 10% coinsurance of remaining balance after copay once
  deductible is met.

### §3.4 Surgical Benefits
All medically necessary inpatient and outpatient surgical procedures covered at **90% plan / 10%
member** after the $750 deductible, with prior authorization. Exclusions in the general policy
exclusions document apply (see exclusions.md).

### §3.5 Inpatient Hospital
- Room and board: 10% coinsurance after deductible (in-network)
- ICU/CCU: 10% coinsurance after deductible
- Skilled nursing facility: 10% coinsurance after deductible, up to 90 days/year

### §3.6 Mental Health and Substance Use
- Inpatient: 10% coinsurance after deductible
- Outpatient/therapy: $20 copay per visit (unlimited visits)

## §4. Prescription Drugs

| Tier | Description | Cost |
|------|-------------|------|
| Tier 1 (Generic) | $5 copay | No deductible |
| Tier 2 (Preferred Brand) | $25 copay | No deductible |
| Tier 3 (Non-Preferred Brand) | $50 copay | No deductible |
| Tier 4 (Specialty) | 10% coinsurance | After deductible; PA required |

---

## §5. Lab and Imaging

- **Diagnostic labs:** 10% coinsurance after deductible (in-network)
- **X-rays:** $25 copay (in-network)
- **MRI / CT / PET:** 10% coinsurance after deductible; prior authorization required

---

## §6. Physical Therapy

Up to **60 sessions per calendar year** at $25 copay per session.
