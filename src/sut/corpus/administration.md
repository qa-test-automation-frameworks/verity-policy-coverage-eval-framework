# HealthGuard Insurance — Plan Administration

_FictiHealth Inc. | Policy Year 2024_

---

## Effective and Expiration Dates

Coverage under all HealthGuard plans (Bronze, Silver, Gold) begins on the enrollment effective
date shown on the member's ID card, and runs through the end of the plan year (December 31) unless
terminated earlier due to non-payment, disenrollment, or loss of eligibility. Coverage does not
apply retroactively to services received before the effective date.

## Plan-Year Changes

Deductibles, out-of-pocket maximums, and accumulator balances (deductible paid, OOP paid) reset to
zero at the start of each new plan year (January 1), regardless of how much was accrued in the
prior plan year. Mid-year plan tier changes (e.g. Bronze to Gold) require a new deductible period;
amounts accrued under the prior tier do not carry over to the new tier.

## Waiting Periods

- **Standard medical and pharmacy benefits:** No waiting period. Coverage is active from the
  effective date for all plans.
- **Orthodontia rider:** 12-month waiting period from rider purchase date before orthodontic
  services are covered.
- **Fertility rider:** 6-month waiting period from rider purchase date.
- **Dental rider (major services):** 6-month waiting period for major services (crowns, bridges,
  root canals); preventive dental cleanings and exams under the rider have no waiting period.

## Riders

Riders are optional add-on coverage purchased separately from the base health plan; the base
exclusions in `exclusions.md` still apply except where a rider explicitly overrides them.

- **Dental Rider:** Adds coverage for routine cleanings/exams (100%, no deductible), fillings (80%
  after $50 deductible), and major services (50% after deductible and waiting period).
- **Vision Rider:** Adds one eye exam per year ($10 copay) and an annual allowance of $150 toward
  eyeglasses or contact lenses.
- **Orthodontia Rider:** Adds coverage for braces and orthodontic treatment at 50% coinsurance up
  to a $2,000 lifetime maximum, subject to the 12-month waiting period above.
- **Fertility Rider:** Adds coverage for IVF, IUI, and egg/sperm freezing at 50% coinsurance up to
  a $10,000 lifetime maximum, subject to the 6-month waiting period above.

## Required Documents for Claims

- **Standard claims:** Itemized provider bill or claim form; no additional documentation required
  for in-network services billed directly by the provider.
- **Out-of-network claims:** Itemized bill, proof of payment, and a completed member claim
  reimbursement form.
- **Prior authorization requests:** Physician's treatment plan or letter of medical necessity, in
  addition to the standard PA request form referenced in `exclusions.md`.
- **Rider claims (dental/vision/orthodontia/fertility):** Standard claim documentation plus the
  applicable rider's enrollment confirmation showing the rider was active on the date of service.

## Network Tiers

HealthGuard uses a two-tier provider network for all plans:

- **Tier 1 (In-Network):** Contracted providers billing at negotiated rates. Standard cost-sharing
  (copay/coinsurance) shown in each plan's benefit summary applies.
- **Tier 2 (Out-of-Network):** Non-contracted providers. Members pay the difference between the
  provider's billed charge and HealthGuard's allowed amount, in addition to standard
  out-of-network cost-sharing. Out-of-network costs do not count toward the in-network
  out-of-pocket maximum; a separate out-of-network OOP maximum of 2x the in-network amount applies
  on all plans.

There is no third "preferred" or "premium" network tier; provider directories listing "Tier 3" are
outdated and should be disregarded in favor of the current two-tier structure above.
