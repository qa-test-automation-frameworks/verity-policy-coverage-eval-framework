# HealthGuard Silver Plan — Benefit Summary

_FictiHealth Inc. | Policy Year 2024_

---

## §1. Overview

The Silver plan balances monthly premiums with moderate cost-sharing. It is FictiHealth's most
popular plan and is eligible for Cost-Sharing Reduction (CSR) subsidies for qualifying members.

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Monthly Premium | $420 |
| Annual Deductible (individual) | $2,000 |
| Annual Deductible (family) | $4,000 |
| Out-of-Pocket Maximum (individual) | $6,000 |
| Out-of-Pocket Maximum (family) | $12,000 |
| Coinsurance (after deductible) | 20% member / 80% plan |

---

## §2. Preventive Care

Preventive services at **100%** with no deductible, in-network. Includes all Bronze preventive
benefits plus:

- Annual nutritional counseling (up to 3 sessions, for medically documented obesity)
- Smoking cessation programs

---

## §3. Medical Benefits

### §3.1 Primary Care Visits
- **In-network:** $30 copay per visit (deductible waived — copay applies from first visit)
- **Out-of-network:** 20% coinsurance after deductible

### §3.2 Specialist Visits
- **In-network:** $60 copay per visit
- **Out-of-network:** 20% coinsurance after deductible

### §3.3 Emergency Room
- **$200 copay** per visit; waived if admitted; 20% coinsurance of remaining balance after copay once
  deductible is met.

### §3.4 Surgical Benefits
All medically necessary inpatient and outpatient surgical procedures covered at **80% plan / 20%
member** after the $2,000 deductible, with prior authorization. Exclusions in the general policy
exclusions document apply (see exclusions.md).

### §3.5 Inpatient Hospital
- Room and board: 20% coinsurance after deductible (in-network)
- ICU/CCU: 20% coinsurance after deductible
- Skilled nursing facility: 20% coinsurance after deductible, up to 90 days/year

### §3.6 Mental Health and Substance Use
- Inpatient: 20% coinsurance after deductible
- Outpatient/therapy: $40 copay per visit (up to 52 visits per year)

### §3.7 Urgent Care
- **In-network urgent care:** $75 copay per visit
- **Out-of-network urgent care:** 20% coinsurance after deductible

| Tier | Description | Cost |
|------|-------------|------|
| Tier 1 (Generic) | $10 copay | No deductible |
| Tier 2 (Preferred Brand) | $40 copay | No deductible |
| Tier 3 (Non-Preferred Brand) | $80 copay | After deductible |
| Tier 4 (Specialty) | 20% coinsurance | After deductible; PA required |

---

## §5. Lab and Imaging

- **Diagnostic labs:** 20% coinsurance after deductible (in-network)
- **X-rays:** $40 copay (in-network)
- **MRI / CT / PET:** 20% coinsurance after deductible; prior authorization required

---

## §6. Physical Therapy

Up to **45 sessions per calendar year** at $40 copay per session.
